Insert node at the head in a doubly linked list
easy

Problem Statement
You are given a Doubly linked list and an integer K . Your task is to insert the integer K at the head of the given linked list

Input
User Task:
Since this will be a functional problem, you don't have to take input. You just have to complete the function insertnew() that takes head node and the integer K as a parameter.

Constraints:
1 <=N<= 1000
1 <=K, value<= 1000

Output
Return the head of the modified linked list

Example
Sample Input:-
5 2
1 2 3 4 5

Sample Output:
2 1 2 3 4 5
