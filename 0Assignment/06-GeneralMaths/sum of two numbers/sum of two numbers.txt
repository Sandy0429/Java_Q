sum of two numbers
easy

Time Limit: 2 sec
Memory Limit: 128000 kB

Problem Statement
You are given three random integers, determine if one of them is sum of the other two.

Input
only line will contains three integers a, b and c.

Constraints
1 <= a, b, c <= 108

Output
print "YES" / "NO".

Example
Input:
2 5 3

output:
YES

Input:
2 5 6

output:
NO
