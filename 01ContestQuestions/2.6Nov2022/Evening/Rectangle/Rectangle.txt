Rectangle
medium

Time Limit: 2 sec
Memory Limit: 128000 kB

Problem Statement
Give integer values M and N. Using the character ‘*’ draw a rectangle of height M and breadth N.

Input
The input contains two integers M and N.

Constraints:-
3 <= M, N <= 20

Output
Print the rectangle as shown in the example

Example
Sample Input:-
3 3

Sample Output:-
***
* *
***

Sample Input:-
5 3

Sample Output:-
***
* *
* *
* *
***
