Area of Square

Time Limit: 2 sec
Memory Limit: 128000 kB

Problem Statement
Given a side of a square, your task is to calculate its area.
Input
The first line of the input contains the side of the square.

Constraints:
1 <= side <=100

Output
You just have to print the area of a square

Example
Sample Input:-
3

Sample Output:-
9

Sample Input:-
6

Sample Output:-
36
